Our focus is client experience

Carton Interiors is a Dublin-based interior design and interior architecture service. We have designed homes in north and south County Dublin, Wicklow, Kildare, Meath and beyond. If you are seeking professional advice on paint colour schemes, furniture layouts, kitchen and bathroom design, lighting solutions and soft furnishings like curtains, blinds and upholstery – look no further. From our studio in Rathfarnham, we prepare and project manage complete interior design schemes, working with you to define your style and create a home with feeling. Our small team has a big passion for interiors!
